About This Blog

Eat What You Love is not just a blog, but a community where food lovers can share their favorite meals, interesting recipes and learn new ones.

There is no wrong or right recipe, so feel free to post anything you love to cook :).

Although we love to share recipes and cooking tips, feel free to share anything else food related.
It could be a picture of a nice meal you cooked, something you would love to cook in the future,
your favorite place to cook at, anything really !
